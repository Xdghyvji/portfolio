Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my services and processes.

What is your typical turnaround time?
Turnaround times vary by project. A standard landing page or basic SMM panel setup typically takes 1-2 weeks. Full custom web applications or detailed SEO audits require 3-4 weeks. We establish a clear timeline during our strategy call.
How does the SMM Panel (Get Grow Up) work?
Our panel is fully automated. Once you select a package (followers, likes, SEO traffic), our API processes the order securely and begins delivery within minutes. We offer an intuitive dashboard for you to track real-time progress.
Do you build on WordPress or custom code?
Both. Depending on your needs, I architect custom solutions using HTML/Tailwind/JavaScript/Python for blazing-fast performance, but I also build heavily customized, easy-to-manage WordPress and Shopify stores for e-commerce clients.
What is your refund policy?
We pride ourselves on 100% client satisfaction. If an SMM order fails to deliver, funds are automatically refunded to your panel balance. For development projects, we work on milestone-based payments to ensure expectations are met at every stage.
